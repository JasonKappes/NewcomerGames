Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11/11/2023

Welcome to Newcomer! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our app. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your personal information.

1. Information Collection and Use
We collect the following types of information:
- Email Address: Used for app functionality and linked to your identity.
- User ID: Assigned to your profile for app functionality and linked to your identity.
- Usage Data: Tracks how you interact with the app, used for improving app functionality.
- Crash Data: Collects information when the app malfunctions, to resolve issues.
- Performance Data: Monitors app performance metrics.
- Other Diagnostic Data: Used for app functionality and may be linked to your identity.
This information is primarily collected for analytics purposes and to enhance app functionality.

2. Data Storage and Security
Your data is securely stored on Google Firebase. Accessing user data requires authentication, ensuring that your information is protected against unauthorized access.

3. Third-Party Data Sharing
We may share your data with third parties, but all personally identifiable information will be removed.

4. User Consent and Control
By using Newcomer, you consent to the collection of the aforementioned data. You may request the deletion of your data by contacting us at jasonkappes656@gmail.com.

5. Children's Privacy
While Newcomer collects data from all users, we only collect email addresses and potentially user IDs as personally identifying information. If you are under 13, please ensure you have parental permission before using our app.

6. International Data Transfer
Newcomer operates globally, and your data may be transferred and processed in countries other than your own. Rest assured, we take all necessary steps to ensure your data is handled securely.

7. Changes to Our Privacy Policy
Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time. However, we will not proactively notify users of these changes. Users are encouraged to review the policy periodically for any updates.

8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our treatment of your personal data, please write to us by email at jasonkappes656@gmail.com.
