How Wisp Works

🎮

1. Pick Your Game

Select any PC title and switch it to your target language.

⌨️

2. Hit Your Hotkeys

Wisp runs in the background, and you can activate it with a hotkey to translate text on screen.

📚

3. Review at your pace

Complete Duolingo-style lessons from the content you've bookmarked.

Deep-Dive: The Four Wisps

Shine (alt+q)

- Understand individual words -

Translate, define, explain, bookmark, and listen to any word

Stream (alt+a)

- Understand phrases and sentences -

Translate, explain, bookmark, and listen to any phrase or sentence

Refresh (alt+w)

- Learn at your pace -

Complete Duolingo-style lessons from the content you've bookmarked

Chronicle (alt+r)

- Review the words you've saved -

View, filter, and practice your bookmarked words and phrases

Why Wisp Is Different

🎮 Game Integration

Wisp works with any game, allowing you to translate text and learn languages inside any videogame

🔍 In-game Overlay

Language information is overlayed on top of your game

⏸️ Universal Pause

Wisp can optionally 'pause' any game, allowing you to read and understand text before continuing

🌐 Language Support

Wisp supports learning and/or translating text across any language pairs

Your Learning Flow

Lessons are generated based on your bookmarked words and phrases, and are designed to be completed at your own pace
